A Little About
Me

Hello, I'm Jason Alvarez, an aspiring Data Analyst with a background in Human Resources and the Behavioral Health field. I have a master's degree in Industrial and Organizational Psychology from The Chicago School of Professional Psychology, where i learned how to apply psychological principles and methods to understand and improve the behavior and performance of individuals and organizations. I have also worked in the Human Resources field for a prominent hopital and healthcare university in Portland, Oregon, where I assisted the Employee Relations department as an HR Coordinator and then shortly worked as an Interim HR Business Partner for the same department.

As an aspiring Data Analyst, I am interested in using data to solve real-world problems and optimize outcomes in various domains, such as employee engagement, customer satisfaction, and organizational development. I have learned, through self-study, data analysis methods, tools and have beginner knowledge of SQL, R, Power BI visualization software, and Tableau visualization software. Additionally, I have plans to learn the Python programming language to increase my data analytical skills.

My goal as Data Analyst is to leverage my knowledge and skills in psychology and Human Resources, as well as further my passion and curiosity for data, and to provide valuable insights and solutions that can help individuals and organizations achieve their goals and objectives. I am always eager to learn new things, challenge myself, and collaborate with others. I am also committed to delivering high-quality work that meets or exceeds expectations.
